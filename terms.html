Terms & Conditions

1. Introduction

Welcome to Petopia. These Terms and Conditions govern your use of our website and services. By booking a service with us, you agree to comply with these terms.

2. Services & Bookings

All appointments for boarding, grooming, and veterinary services are subject to availability. We reserve the right to refuse service to any pet that poses a danger to our staff or other animals.

  • Appointments: Prior booking is recommended. Walk-ins are subject to slot availability.
  • Cancellations: Please provide at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Failure to do so may incur a fee.

3. Pet Health & Safety

To ensure the safety of all pets in our care:

  • Vaccinations: All pets must have up-to-date vaccination records.
  • Health: Pets must be free from contagious diseases, fleas, and ticks.
  • Aggression: Owners must disclose any history of aggression. We reserve the right to muzzle or isolate pets if necessary for safety.
  • Zero Cuts Policy: We generally avoid shaving double-coated dogs unless medically necessary, as it can damage their fur and temperature regulation.

4. Liability

While we exercise extreme care, Petopia is not liable for:

  • Loss or damage to personal items (leashes, collars, toys) left with the pet.
  • Any underlying health conditions that may flare up during the stay.
  • Unforeseeable accidents, although we take every precaution to prevent them.

5. Rights of Admission

Management reserves the right to decline or terminate services if a pet is deemed unmanageable, aggressive, or if the terms are violated.
