Privacy Policy

At Petopia, accessible from our premises and website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and customers. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Petopia and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you book an appointment, register on our site, or subscribe to our newsletter. This includes:

  • Name and Contact Information (Phone, Email, Address)
  • Pet Details (Name, Breed, Age, Medical History)
  • Payment Information (for transaction processing only)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • Process your bookings and communicate with you regarding appointments.
  • Send you emails or updates regarding special offers or policy changes.
  • Ideally, to understand and analyze how you use our website to improve user experience.

3. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Partners

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and trusted affiliates.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
